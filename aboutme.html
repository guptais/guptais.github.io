Something about me

Writing code & drinking tea makes me a happy person. I am a post graduate in Applied Electronics and Theoretical Physics from University of Delhi, India. I studied Quantum Computers in university, which was very interesting then as well. I learnt coding from Pascal, moved to code in C, C++, C#, Java, Javascript and now Python. Technology is a life skill these days. Even if you are not in technical space, having some understanding of these things, can come really handy; And with the kind of online resources available, anybody can learn and start a new career or upskill themselves.
